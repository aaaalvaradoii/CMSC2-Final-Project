About us

    The Society of Applied Mathematics of UPLB, or SAM-UP, is a duly-recognized, student academic organization based in the University of the Philippines Los Baños. The organization’s primary objective is to promote applied mathematics and its related fields to the university, and ultimately, the Philippine society.

    On November 27, 1984, 28 applied mathematics majors formed SAM-UP, in their common interests and regards. Over the course of 31 years, the Society grew to 80 batches after the founding members. Several events such as the Inter-Bloc Mathematics Quiz Contest (IBMQ), Math in Action (MIA), Numero were instituted to promote applied mathematics to the community outside the organization, among others.

Objectives

    In pursuit of its aims, the organization do hereby seek:

  • To promote awareness of the relevance of applied mathematics to the Philippine society;
  • To contribute to the advancement of the Philippine society through deeper appreciation and wider application of applied mathematics and its related fields;
  • To help the University in the achievement of its purposes and missions;
  • To promote academic excellence among its members; and
  • To help in the moral upliftment and social awareness and development of its members.

Major Achievments

    In a span of 31 years, the Society has excelled in and out of the University through its events and members. Here are some of SAM-UP’s accomplishments:

  • SAM-UP alumni spread worldwide (Singapore, Taiwan, USA, UK, Middle East, etc.); encompassing fields of operations research and actuarial science, and in other related disciplines.
  • Math in Action (MIA) is the only nationwide high school mathematics camp conducted in and by UPLB constituents (SAM-UP).
  • SAM-UP has garnered many PalaCASan accolades: 9 Cheerdance Competitions championships, 11 Cheerdance Competitions podium finishes, and many more.
    The organization, not only has material accomplishments, but also considers the stronghold of more than 400 members as a major triumph and success. SAM-UP, for one, focuses on member self-development and we believe that through this venture on human resources, our organization is and will be more prosperous in the succeeding years.
