IBMQ

Inter-Bloc Mathematics Quiz, or IBMQ, is a math quiz contest open for new freshmen of UPLB. It aims to enhance the students' mathematical skills, to develop teamwork among their blocs, and to expose them in a more competitive atmosphere. The competition covers arithmetic, highschool algebra, plane geometry, solid geometry, trigonometry, logic, combinatorics, and probability.




Math Quest

Math Quest is SAM-UP's biggest event in the organization. It consists of two events held annualy alternately.



Math in Action


Math in Action, or MIA, is a recreational mathematics camp open to high schools nationwide. It is a two-day activity where students from different schools will be engaged to activities which will enhance their understanding and appreciation of Mathematics and its application to the society.



Inter Elementary Math Quiz


Inter Elementary Mathematics Quiz, or IEMQ, is a quiz competition open to all elementary students nationwide. It aims to enhance students' understanding and appreciation of Mathematics.
